Dream Meanings

Discover the possible meanings behind common dreams and understand the symbols hidden in your imagination.

Flying Dreams

Flying dreams are often connected with freedom, confidence and the desire to overcome challenges.

Falling Dreams

Falling dreams may represent feelings of uncertainty, stress or a situation that feels out of control.

Water Dreams

Water dreams are commonly associated with emotions, change and personal growth.

Snake Dreams

Snake dreams can symbolize good luck,transformation, hidden fears or important changes in life.

Chasing Dreams

Being chased in a dream may reflect worries, pressure or something you are trying to avoid.

Lost Dreams

Dreams about being lost may represent confusion or searching for direction in life.
