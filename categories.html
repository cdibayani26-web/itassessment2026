Dream Categories

Explore different types of dreams and discover the themes that appear in our imagination.

Nature Dreams

Dreams about forests, oceans, mountains, rivers and natural environments.

Animal Dreams

Dreams involving animals such as birds, butterflies, snakes and wild creatures.

Fantasy Dreams

Dreams filled with magical worlds, imaginary places and mysterious experiences.

Emotional Dreams

Dreams connected with feelings like happiness, fear, sadness and excitement.

Nightmare Dreams

Dreams that involve fear, danger or stressful situations.

Adventure Dreams

Dreams about travelling, exploring and discovering new experiences.
